AdvaRisk & the EO Downstream Thesis

Technical addendum — how industry thinking (SatSure / EO application layer) maps to this codebase · {{ generated_at }}

Core thesis (EO industry): More satellites do not automatically improve decisions. The binding constraint is turning pixels into answers that fit how a customer decides — orchestration, context, and decision-ready modelling. AdvaRisk is built in that direction, not as a data-vendor stack.

1. Where value is created

Satellite catalogs (GEE: S2, S1, Dynamic World) ↓ data_loader.process_parcel_data() Orchestration + fusion + QA (merge, masks, extract_timeseries) ↓ analytics_engine.run_full_analytics_pipeline() Context + rules (weather, land cover, cycles, guardband) ↓ Decision outputs (verdict, activity %, confidence, PDF annexure) ↓ api.py → pdf_generator → lender-facing report

Upstream (~20% of product code): fetch indices via Earth Engine. Downstream (~80%): smoothing, empirical logic, seasonal tables, environmental veto, PDF/API packaging.

5. Gaps vs “essential workflow” (pilot → production)

  • QA: no minimum clear-pixel fraction per parcel before accepting monsoon NDVI scenes.
  • Semantics: 15% activity threshold not yet co-defined with risk teams as score bands.
  • Integration: PDF/API output, not wired into loan origination or audit trail standards.
  • Context breadth: no socio-economic / ground-truth layers in pipeline yet.

6. Where the moat should sit (for this team)

Not the moat: downloading more Sentinel scenes.
The moat: (1) trusted orchestration + QA, (2) sector-specific fusion and rules banks accept, (3) embedding in their decision engine so switching cost is high. Compounding comes from each deployment improving pipelines and the next sector shipping faster.

Answer to “data vs models vs decision integration?” — For AdvaRisk, lasting advantage aligns with decision integration + contextual fusion; satellite data and heuristics/models are enablers. Next engineering priority: tighten downstream honesty (cloud QA, optical vs SAR separation) and co-design verdict/score meaning with customers — not add indices alone.
